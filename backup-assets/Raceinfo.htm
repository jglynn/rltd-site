Race Info

The 1st annual Read Like The Dickens race will take place on June 27th 2009. The race will begin on the starting line at the Elko speedway and will continue through town, and then finish back on the speedway. Awards will be given for the top 3 finishers, both male and female, in all age brackets. There are also many other prizes for best female and best male times as well as oldest and youngest runners. Thanks to our wonderful sponsors, Vacation Sports will be conducting the official timing of the race, There will also be various items on silent auction, all proceeds will benefit the New Market Library.
