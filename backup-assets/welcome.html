Tonight we had the pleasure of attending the Elko New Market City Council meeting where we presented a few comments on Kim's love for reading including her flashlight under the covers reading as a child, after she was told lights out.  We also mentioned that she provided Zeke and Ellie with a flashlight of their own! 

 
 I informed the council that we had anticipated 30 or 40 runners, and hoped to raise a few hundred dollars in her honor and loving memory. We then presented a check for twenty five hundred dollars! to the Library Director for Scott County, Vanessa Birdsey. This is a photo taken outside the council meeting after the original presentation.
 
 I have asked that the be money earmarked for our library here in town and used for something tangible. We then discussed possibly getting a literacy computer for our library and buying some books with the remaining funds. We also discussed developing more reading programs with a host guest as well.
 
We are on board for doing it again next year and are definitely on cloud nine with this years success.
Thank you all for your support and generosity.
 lovingly,

 John, Zeke and Ellie

August 14th 2008
